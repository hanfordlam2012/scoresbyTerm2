Term 2, 2021 - Student Name

This project is about combining online research with HTML, CSS, and JS concepts to deliver a website. ( Imagine that this is what you have been hired as a web developer to do for your customer! ) You may work in groups of 3 to put together this website. Sketch out possible page designs with pen and paper. Begin by researching a focus topic related to "The Web" and think about what information you would like to present. Examples include: "History of the Web", "How the Internet Works", "Google's PageRank Algorithm", "Online Security", "Impact of Internet Gaming", "Pros and Cons of Decentralisation", "Client and Server-side Web Development", "The Internet of Things (IoT)", "Smart Devices", etc.
